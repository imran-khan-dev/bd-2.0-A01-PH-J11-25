Pillars of Bangladesh 2.0

Innovation

Drives growth through technology, enabling new industries and job creation.

Sustainability

Focuses on green energy, waste management, and protecting resources for future generations.

Infrastructure

Improves connectivity and trade, supporting economic development and quality of life.

Empowerment

Promotes inclusive growth, education, gender equality, and economic independence for all.

Vision of Future

Bangladesh 2.0 aims to redefine the nation’s trajectory by embracing cutting-edge technology, promoting inclusive growth, and ensuring sustainability. With initiatives in renewable energy, digital transformation, and robust infrastructure, the country is set to become a global model of development.

Digital Transformation

Creating a nationwide system to improve connectivity and service access for all citizens.

Green Energy

Investing in solar and wind power for a greener, eco-friendly future.

Modern Infrastructure

Developing advanced infrastructure to foster economic growth and global competitiveness.
